# OperationPlantBased.com — Design Brainstorm

## Approach A — "Field Notes"
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Organic Editorial / Field Journal
**Core Principles:** Handcrafted warmth, earthy authority, beginner-welcoming clarity, mission-driven urgency
**Color Philosophy:** Deep forest green (#1C3A2B) as the anchor — conveys nature, health, and seriousness without the cliché of lime green wellness brands. Warm cream (#F5EFE0) as the background, giving a paper-journal feel. Terracotta (#C4622D) as the action accent — energetic, food-adjacent, memorable.
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric editorial grid. Hero sections offset text left with a large image bleeding off the right edge. Cards stagger in a masonry-influenced layout. Sections use alternating left/right content blocks to create rhythm.
**Signature Elements:** Thin botanical line-art dividers between sections. Handwritten-style display font for headlines. A "mission badge" stamp graphic used as a recurring motif.
**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal a subtle paper-texture overlay. CTAs animate with a gentle upward nudge. Progress steps in the Mission Planner feel like filling out a field journal.
**Animation:** Entrance animations use a slow fade-up (300ms ease-out). Section transitions use a horizontal wipe. Form steps slide in from the right.
**Typography System:** Display — Playfair Display (bold, italic for emphasis). Body — Source Serif 4 (regular, 18px). UI labels — DM Sans (medium, uppercase tracking).
</text>

## Approach B — "Clean Ops"
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Modern Wellness Ops / Functional Minimalism
**Core Principles:** Clarity over decoration, system-thinking aesthetics, professional credibility, conversion-optimized whitespace
**Color Philosophy:** Off-white (#FAFAF8) background. Slate charcoal (#1E2A22) for text — not pure black, slightly warm. Sage green (#5C7A5E) as the primary brand color — calm, credible, not aggressive. Amber (#E8A838) as the conversion accent for CTAs only.
**Layout Paradigm:** Structured asymmetric columns. Hero uses a 60/40 split — copy left, image right. Sections use a consistent left-rail label system (small uppercase category labels) that creates a magazine-like navigation feel.
**Signature Elements:** Thin horizontal rule separators with category labels. A "10-week progress tracker" visual motif. Clean icon system using outlined botanical icons.
**Interaction Philosophy:** Everything responds instantly. Buttons scale 0.97 on press. Hover states shift background by one shade. The Mission Planner feels like a clean intake form, not a quiz.
**Animation:** Minimal. Fade-up on scroll (200ms). No decorative motion. Progress bar fills smoothly on quiz steps.
**Typography System:** Display — Fraunces (variable, wght 700). Body — Inter (400/500, 16px). Labels — Space Grotesk (600, small caps).
</text>

## Approach C — "Harvest Table" ← SELECTED
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Warm Brutalist Editorial / Farmhouse Modern
**Core Principles:** Bold typographic hierarchy, warm tactile textures, food-first visual language, confident but approachable
**Color Philosophy:** Warm white (#FFFDF7) background. Deep olive (#2D3B2A) as the primary text and nav color — rich, earthy, authoritative. Burnt sienna (#B85C38) as the primary action color — food-adjacent, warm, high-contrast. Pale sage (#C8D5C0) as a secondary surface color for cards and section backgrounds.
**Layout Paradigm:** Oversized typographic hero with a large food/lifestyle image in a cropped circle or organic blob shape. Sections use a bold left-column label system. Product cards use a tall portrait ratio. The Mission Planner uses a full-screen step layout.
**Signature Elements:** Large bold section numbers (01, 02, 03) as decorative anchors. A recurring leaf/sprout icon mark. Thick horizontal rules in olive that act as visual anchors.
**Interaction Philosophy:** CTAs feel decisive — they fill with color on hover rather than just changing border. The Mission Planner steps feel like turning pages. Testimonials slide horizontally.
**Animation:** Bold entrance: headlines animate in word-by-word (stagger 40ms). Cards fan in from bottom (150ms stagger). CTA buttons fill left-to-right on hover (200ms).
**Typography System:** Display — Playfair Display (900 weight, tight tracking). Subheadings — DM Serif Display (italic). Body — Lato (400, 17px). UI — DM Sans (500).
</text>
